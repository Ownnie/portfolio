## Technical challenges solved

### Medical eligibility engine (MEC)

Implementation of the medical eligibility criteria algorithm based on WHO, ACOG, and MinSalud guidelines:

- **1–4 Categorization**: Automatic classification system that evaluates the safety of each method according to specific conditions
- **Complex dynamic rules**: 
  - Postpartum/breastfeeding with intervals (less than 21, 21–42, more than 42 days) and VTE risk
  - Smoking ≥35 years with a threshold of 15 cigarettes/day (category 3 vs 4 for CHC)
  - Migraine with/without aura, severe hypertension, DVT/PE, thrombophilias, vascular diabetes
  - Septic post-abortion, heavy bleeding, and 40+ conditions assessed
- **Smart conditional flow**: Questions adapt based on previous answers (age, pregnancy, nulliparity, etc.)
- **Modular architecture**: Clear separation between `engine.ts` (evaluation logic), `mappings.ts` (clinical rules), and `questions.ts` (questionnaire)

### Privacy & local-only data handling

Privacy-focused design without backend infrastructure:

- **Exclusive LocalStorage**: All test answers are stored only in the user's browser
- **Robust consent**: Modal with two checkboxes (medical notice + ≥14 years verification) persisted locally
- **No tracking**: Zero personal identifiers, third-party cookies, or data sent to servers
- **Privacy page**: Clear documentation of data handling and legal disclaimers

### JSON-driven content architecture

Scalable and maintainable system for managing medical information:

- **Data & UI separation**: Method sheets in structured JSON (`/content/methods/*.json`)
- **Easy versioning**: Medical content changes without touching code
- **Ready for i18n**: Structure ready for internationalization
- **Dynamic images**: Mapping system to associate images by method (`METHOD_IMAGES`)

### Next.js 16 technical fixes

Migration and optimization for modern App Router:

- **Async params**: Fix for accessing `params` as Promise in dynamic routes
- **Viewport export**: Separation of viewport configuration according to new APIs
- **Dynamic metadata**: Async `generateMetadata` for per-method SEO
- **Node >=20.9**: Runtime and dependencies updated

## Main features

### Method sheets (`/methods`)

- **List with search and filters**: Hormonal, Non-hormonal, LARC, Emergency
- **12 methods covered**: IUD (Cu/LNG), Implant (3/5 years), Injectables (monthly/quarterly), Pills (combined/progestin-only), Patch, Ring, Barrier, Emergency pill, Natural methods
- **Info cards**: Badges, brief description, representative image
- **Detailed method sheets**: Quick facts, efficacy (typical/perfect), advantages, considerations, CTA to test

### Interactive MEC test (`/test`)

- **Adaptive questionnaire**: 40+ conditional questions based on health profile
- **Real-time evaluation**: Instant classification of all methods (1–4)
- **Personalized results**: Recommendations based on medical evidence
- **Local persistence**: State saved in browser to resume later

### Method comparator (`/compare`)

- **Multiple selection**: Up to 3 methods simultaneously
- **Responsive table**: Side-by-side comparison of key features
- **Compared criteria**: Type/hormones, duration, STI protection, efficacy, emergency use, advantages and considerations
- **Optimized UX**: Selectable pills, highlighted headers, informative badges

### Home & navigation

- **Impactful hero**: Clear CTA to the test
- **Educational sections**: Objective, need, clinical question PICO
- **Consistent header/footer**: Main navigation, educational/emergency notice
- **Coherent design**: Pink + white palette, gradients, underbar, hover cards

## UX & content improvements

- **Brand-free copy**: "Emergency pill" instead of brand names
- **Dual copper IUD**: Marked as regular contraceptive AND emergency method
- **Visible barrier methods**: With clear note about STI protection
- **Natural methods included**: Full coverage of contraceptive spectrum
- **Mobile-first**: Simple controls, readable fonts, generous spacing
- **Accessibility**: Clear badges, proper contrast, intuitive navigation

## Impact & results

- **Evidence-based education**: Up-to-date information according to international guidelines (WHO/ACOG/MinSalud)
- **Informed autonomy**: Users can explore options according to their specific health profile
- **Guaranteed privacy**: Zero risk of sensitive health data leakage
